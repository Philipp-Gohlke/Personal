Philipp Gohlke

Research Associate in Mathematics

Currently, I am employed by the Friedrich Schiller University Jena, working in the research group of Prof. Tobias Jäger . My research is focused on the theory of dynamical systems, especially symbolic dynamics. I am particularly interested in problems concerning systems with aperiodic order, multifractal analysis and Schrödinger operators.

I obtained my PhD from Bielefeld University in 2021, under the supervision of Prof. Michael Baake and Prof. David Damanik (Rice University, Houston). From 2022 to 2024 I held a Walter Benjamin Fellowship by the German Research Foundation (DFG) at Lund University (Sweden).
